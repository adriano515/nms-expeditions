Step 3: Download

Save to the appropriate directory:

Steam PC:
%APPDATA%\HelloGames\NMS\{steam id}\cache
Steam Mac:
~/Library/Application Support/HelloGames/NMS/cache
MS Store/GOG:
%APPDATA%\HelloGames\NMS\cache
SteamDeck:
/home/deck/.steam/steam/steamapps/compatdata/275850/pfx/drive_c/Users/SteamUser/AppData/Roaming/HelloGames/NMS/cache

Note: These directories may be somewhat different on your machine.

You must be OFFLINE (meaning Steam needs to be in offline mode, or you need to disconnect from the internet) when you start NMS for this to work, otherwise your expedition JSON will be overwritten and any offline expeditions you were on will end when you load the save.

Notes

  • Most of these expeditions start with tech in inventory slots. You need to move them.
  • Some expeditions require us to "auto complete" certain milestones as you cannot complete them offline. We're still trying to figure out a better way to do this other than just giving the reward.
  • If you need Polo to show you Atlas stations, he won't do it UNLESS you also ask him to show you black holes. Then you will see both on your galaxy map.

01: Pioneers (Redux)

  • You start on a green star and get the green star milestone right away. This seems wrong. Not sure if it's fixable in the JSON, could be that the star flipped green in an update.

12: Omega

  • All the optional missions can't be done offline. They are trivial once you get to the planet, so unlocking them for "free" seems fine.
